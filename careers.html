Design

  • Lead Designer

    Pave Overview

    Pave is a NYC based startup reinventing financial products for people with limited credit histories. Traditional underwriting is based on a person’s history versus their potential. At Pave we look at a number of signals in addition to one's credit history (e.g., expected future income, education, hire-ability) to offer lower, more personalized rates (e.g., payment flexibility, grace periods, etc.), while also delivering a best in class and more human experience.

    The Role

    As the Lead Designer at Pave you’ll work with a passionate and innovative team of engineers, product managers and marketers in a fast-paced, user-centric environment. You will bring enthusiasm, invention, and creativity to the role, coupled with a strong desire to solve problems with our users in mind.

    Responsibilities:

    • Design innovative, elegant and user friendly products
    • Create designs and optimize layouts and landing pages
    • Work closely with product managers, engineers and marketers
    • Conceptualize and produce dynamic design applications including websites and marketing materials (on and offline).
    • Manage several large projects simultaneously

    Qualifications:

    • 5+ years of experience as a designer on a consumer focused product
    • Strong portfolio that demonstrates problem solving skills and attention to detail
    • You’ve shipped products: you know the challenges (and rewards!) of release cycles and how to partner across the organization at every stage, shipping quickly and at the highest level of quality
    • Detailed understanding of web UI standards, usability, and have developed user interfaces for web applications
    • Able to work and thrive in a fast-paced team environment and flexible to accommodate demanding project schedules
    • Direct to consumer experience
    • Adobe Creative Suite
    • Detailed understanding of web UX/UI standards, usability, and have developed user interfaces for web applications

    Compensation and Perks:

    • Compensation will be competitive and commensurate with experience.
    • Health benefits, 401K, paid vacation plus major holidays.
    • Awesome start-up environment in the heart of the West Soho, NYC.
    • Frequent company events with a smart and super fun team.
    • Opportunity to be a part of a transformative startup disrupting consumer lending and personal finance.

    If you’re interested in this role please email careers@pave.com.
