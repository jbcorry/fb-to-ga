Online Privacy Policy

Welcome to Pave, the online service of Pave, Inc. and its affiliates ("Pave," "we," or "us"). Our Online Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information collected on www.pave.com ("Site"), and your choices about the collection and use of your information. Information is collected on the Site by Pave, Inc. in its capacity as (i) servicer and administrator to Pave GP and certain other third party loan purchasers on whose behalf it is acting, and (ii) as an agent of Cross River Bank for whom we act as marketer and servicer of the loan program. By continuing to use this Site, you agree to this Policy. This Policy applies only to the use of our Site and does not apply to information we collect by other means including the Borrower Platform agreement and other loan documents.

1. How We Collect and Use Information

When you visit our site, we may collect the following types of information about you:

Personal Information You Provide: When you use our Site, we may collect the following types of personal information you provide us through forms and questionnaires on our Site. This information can include your name, address, date of birth, telephone number, housing information, income, work history, driver's license, passport or government issued ID and social security number.

If you choose to use the "Instant Bank Verification" service, you will be asked to provide, in addition to the above, the log-in credentials to your bank accounts accounts.

Third Party Sources: We may receive information about you from public and commercially available sources, as permitted by law, and may combine this information with other information we receive about you. For example, if you log in through or use social media functionality embedded in the Site, we may receive information about you from those social media services.

Analytics information: We may directly collect analytics data, or use third-party analytics tools, to help us measure traffic and usage trends for the Site. These tools collect information sent by your browser or device, including the pages you visit and other information that assists us in improving the Site. We aggregate this information so that it cannot be manipulated to identify any particular user.

Cookies information: Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by the websites you visit. These text files can be read by websites to help to identify you when you return. When you visit our Site, we may send one or more cookies to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser to enhance your navigation through the Site. A cookie may also convey information to us about how you use the Site (e.g., the pages you view, the links you click and other actions you take on the Site), and allow us or our business partners to track your usage of the Site over time. There are two types of cookies: persistent cookies and session cookies. A persistent cookie remains on your computer after you have signed offline. Persistent cookies may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Site. Persistent cookies can be removed by following your web browser’s directions. A session cookie is temporary and disappears after you close your browser. You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the Site may not function properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled. To find out how to see what cookies have been set and how to reject and delete the cookies, please visit: http://www.aboutcookies.org.

Clear gifs/web beacons information: When you use the Site, we may employ clear gifs (also known as web beacons) which are used to anonymously track the online usage patterns of our users. In addition, we may also use clear gifs in HTML-based emails sent to our users to track which emails are opened and which links are clicked by recipients. The information allows for more accurate reporting and improvement of the Site. We may also use this information to provide you with more personalized advertising experiences. You may choose to opt-out of some of this tracking through the links provided in the "Your Choices" section of this Policy.

Log file information: Log file information is automatically reported by your browser or device each time you access our Site. When you use our Site, our servers automatically record certain log file information. These server logs may include anonymous information such as your web request, Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, referring / exit pages and URLs, number of clicks and how you interact with links on the Site, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed, and other such information.

Device identifiers: When you access the Site by or through a mobile device (including but not limited to smart-phones or tablets), we may access, collect, monitor and/or remotely store one or more "device identifiers," such as a universally unique identifier ("UUID"). Device identifiers are small data files or similar data structures stored on or associated with your mobile device, which uniquely identify your device. A device identifier may be data stored in connection with the device hardware, data stored in connection with the device’s operating system or other software, or data sent to the device by Pave. A device identifier may convey information to us about how you browse and use the Site. A device identifier may remain persistently on your device to enhance your navigation through the Site. Some features of the Site may not function properly if use or availability of device identifiers is impaired or disabled.

Commercial and marketing communications: We use the information we collect or receive to communicate directly with you. We may send you e-mails containing newsletters, promotions and special offers. If you do not want to receive such email messages, you will be given the option to opt out of such communications as set forth in the "Your Choices" section below. We also use your information to send you service-related emails (e.g., purchase and billing confirmations and reminders, changes/updates to features of the service, and technical and security notices). You may not opt out of service-related e-mails.

2. How we share your information

All financial companies need to share customers’ personal information to run their everyday business. We may share your personal information as follows:

  • If you apply for a loan, with the creditor, currently Cross River Bank, who originates all the loans on the Site and any investor who purchases 100% of your loan.
  • With service providers who help with parts of our business operations such as authenticating the identity of potential borrowers, fraud prevention, verification of the information contained in your account, bill collection, marketing and technology services, and financial institutions that we partner with to offer a product and/or support our servicing activities or to improve such third parties' services. Our contracts dictate that these third parties only use your information in connection with the services they perform for us.
  • If you elect to use the Instant Bank Verification service we use to authenticate the identity of potential borrowers, we may share your bank account information and log-in credentials with the third party which provides this service for us.
  • With credit bureaus to report account information, as permitted by law.
  • With law enforcement, government officials, or other third parties when (1) we are compelled to do so by a subpoena, court order or similar legal procedure, (2) we need to do so to comply with law, or (3) if we believe in good faith that the disclosure of your personal information is necessary to prevent physical harm or financial loss or to report suspected illegal activity. (4) to enforce our Terms of Use or to protect the security or integrity of our Site and services; and/or (5) to exercise or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Pave, our Users or others.
  • For applicants taking courses: The course for which you are using the funds. This information will be limited to your name, email address, state of residency, and funding status.
  • With other third parties but only if you consent or direct us to do so.

Pave will not sell or rent any of your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes and only shares your personal information with third parties as described above. However, we may share information that has been made anonymous through de-identification or aggregation ("De-Identified Data") and we do not limit our third party providers from using, selling, licensing, distributing or disclosing De-Identified Data.

If you are a Pave customer, Federal and state laws allow you to restrict the sharing of your personal information in certain instances such as sharing your information so that third parties can market to you.

Change of Control: We may buy or sell/divest/transfer the company (including any shares in the company), or any combination of its products, services, assets and/or businesses. Your information such as customer names and email addresses and other user information related to the Site may be among the items sold or otherwise transferred in these types of transactions. We may also sell, assign or otherwise transfer such information in the course of corporate divestitures, mergers, acquisitions, bankruptcies, dissolutions, reorganizations, liquidations, similar transactions or proceedings involving all or a portion of the company. This includes a transfer to any back-up service provider or back-up administrator we or Pave GP engage in the event we stop operating.

Pave may transfer information that we collect about you, including personal information, to affiliated entities, or to other third parties across borders to other countries or jurisdictions around the world.

Keeping your information safe: Pave cares about the security of your information, and uses commercially reasonable safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of all information collected through the Site. However, Pave cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to Pave or guarantee that information on or provided through the Site may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed.

4. Your Choices About Your Information

Email: You can stop receiving promotional email communications from us by clicking on the "unsubscribe link" provided in such communications. We make every effort to promptly process all unsubscribe requests. As noted above, you may not opt out of service-related communications (e.g., purchase and billing confirmations and reminders, changes/updates to features of the service, and technical and security notices). If you have any questions about service-related communications or unsubscribing from Pave communications, you can contact us directly at team@pave.com

Your Online User Account: You may access your Online User Account to view or correct the personal information you provided to us and which is associated with your Online User Account. You may request we delete your Online User Account by writing us at team@pave.com. If you choose to delete your Online User Account, we begin the process of deleting your Online User Account from our systems. We will retain your personal information until the earlier of (i) the information is no longer necessary to accomplish the purpose for which it was provided; or (ii) you delete your Online User Account as stated above. We may retain your information for longer periods to the extent that we are obliged to do so in accordance with applicable laws and regulations and/or we reasonably believe that retaining the information is necessary to protect our legal rights or legitimate business interests. Even if you delete your Online User Account, keep in mind that the deletion by our third party providers may not be immediate and that the deleted information may persist in backup copies for a reasonable period of time. Also, information that had already been shared with third parties may not be deleted by them. For any privacy or data-protection-related questions, please write to team@pave.com.

Analytics: You can control the information provided to Google and opt out of certain ads provided by Google by using the methods set forth at www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/". You can opt-out of Mixpanel’s automatic retention of data collected through your browsers by clicking www.mixpanel.com/optout/ . To track opt-outs Mixpanel uses a persistent opt-out cookie placed on your devices. Mixpanel opt-out cookies will not prevent any other data collection methods. If you get a new computer, install a new browser, erase or otherwise alter your browser's cookie file (including upgrading certain browsers) they may also clear the Mixpanel opt-out cookie.

Advertising: If you do not wish to receive targeted advertising based on your use of our Site, you may use publicly available tools that may help you understand which third parties have currently enabled cookies for your browser or mobile device and how to opt-out of some of those cookies. You may access these tools at: www.aboutads.info/choices/ and www.networkadvertising.org/choices from your browser and from your mobile device.

If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at team@pave.com

Our Site does not support Do Not Track at this time. For all the details, including how to turn on Do Not Track, visit www.donottrack.us.
