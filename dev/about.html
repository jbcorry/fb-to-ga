Our Mission

Pave believes we all deserve the opportunity to succeed.

We offer you loans by looking at who you are and your future potential.

Based in New York City, Pave is on the mission to help you succeed by removing the obstacles that get in your way: lack of access to affordable funding. We've started with the Pave Loan.
Our technology enables us to give people looking to advance themselves an affordable loan by looking beyond their credit score to who they are and where they're going.
